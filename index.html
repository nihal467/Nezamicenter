Hijama Therapy

The word hijama means drawing out in Arabic. It is a safe, non-invasive and economical way of curing and preventing many diseases. it is now being recoganized as an alternative medicine or alternative way of treating different bodily disorders.

Agni karma is an important para-surgical measure extensively practiced in ayurveda. It comes under shalya tantra [surgical branch of ayurveda].Its simple, ambulatory , minimal invasive and day-care procedure, it can be utilized as a preventive.
